How the Lamberts Index Works
The same statistical edge logic as Smartodds/Matthew Benham's network — the model behind Brentford and FC Midtjylland. Outsider edge via proprietary modelling, applied to recruitment decisions.
📐
LI Score (0–100)
Position-specific percentile rank built from SQS blueprints — weighted metric sets (6–10 KPIs per position) calibrated to elite performance benchmarks. 80+ = top quintile globally.
⚡
Lamberts Index
LI Score Rank minus Market Value Rank. Positive = undervalued. A player ranked #50 by output but #200 by price scores +150. Hidden gems score +30 or more.
🎯
vs Hradec
LI Score delta against Hradec's position benchmark. Positive = clear upgrade. The scouting filter that converts global data into club-specific decisions.
